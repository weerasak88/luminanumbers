Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 26, 2026

At Lumina Numbers, your privacy is sacred to us — just like your numbers. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how we protect it.

What We Collect

We collect minimal data to improve your experience:

  • Analytics data — Pages visited, time on site, general location (country/city level). Collected only with your consent via Google Analytics 4.
  • No personal numerology data — Birth dates you enter are calculated entirely in your browser. We never see, store, or transmit them.
  • No account data — We don't require sign-up or login.

Cookies

We use cookies only for analytics purposes, and only after you have given explicit consent. You can decline cookies and all tools will still work fully.

  • lumina_cookie_consent — Stores your cookie preference (accepted/declined). Expires after 1 year.
  • Google Analytics (_ga, _gid) — Only set if you accept cookies. Used to understand aggregate site usage. IP addresses are anonymized.

To withdraw consent at any time, clear your browser's localStorage and cookies for luminanumbers.com.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Analytics 4 — Used for anonymous site analytics. Google Privacy Policy →
  • Google Fonts — Fonts are loaded from Google's servers. Google may log requests. Google Privacy Policy →
  • Cloudflare — Our hosting provider. Cloudflare may process request metadata for security. Cloudflare Privacy Policy →

Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Union, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

Since we store no personal numerology data, the only data potentially held is anonymized analytics data collected after consent.

Children's Privacy

Lumina Numbers is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? You can reach us through the contact page at luminanumbers.com/contact
